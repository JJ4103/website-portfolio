Zero Clothing Branding

Role

  • Branding, Social Media Management, Art Director

Project Type

  • Branding and Merchandise Design

Tools Used

  • Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Indesign

Team

  • Jainesh Jadav - Brand Designer and Social Media Manager
  • Keshvi - Merchandise Designer

Duration

  • October 2023- March 2024

The goal of this project was to create branding materials for “ZERO : A clothing brand which is a fusion of streetwear and anime inspired clothes”. The project includes design of brand material such as Logo, Typography, Brand Guidelines, Brand Voice and clothing Design.

Design Strategy


I divided the Design Startegy into 4 sections. The first one is the research phase where I did research on the current market and competitors. The second one is the ideating and sketching phase where I started creating my brand logo, the third is the design phase where I turn my sketches into digital design and the fourth is where i take inspiration from various clothing platforms and design the merchandise.
