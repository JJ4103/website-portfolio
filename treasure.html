Re-designed the Fine-cooking Magazine

Role

  • Typographer, Art Director

Project Type

  • Editorial Design

Tools Used

  • Adobe InDesign

Team

  • Jainesh Jadav - Designer and Art Director

Duration

  • September 2023 - Dec 2023

The goal of this project was to re-design Fine-cooking Magazine with proper layout and imaginary while keeping the printing process in mind. The project includes heavy ideation and research process.

Design Strategy


For my design startegy and layout I decided to use a 12 coloumn grid. Moreover, I thought of having different layout of coloumns for each page. For example, some pages have a 2 coloumn layout whereas pages with more information have 3 column layout. Furthermore, to give the reader a break I decided to use more pictures rather than the normal layout of “FINE COOKING” magazine.So, I decided to use images even on the pages with text. Just small images of the advertisement or topic related item which can give the reader a break from the reading and keep them interested in the magazine.
